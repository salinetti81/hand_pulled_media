Carlos Ledesma is a filmmaker, actor and music composer. His latest short film "Leo's Shoulder" was official selection, among others, at the Buenos Aires International Independent Film Festival (BAFICI) as well as at the New Filmmakers Film Festival New York edition. He was also nominated in the Best New Director category at the Maipu Shorts Film Festival, in Buenos Aires. His previous short, "Take it Easy, Charlie" was part of the Cortocircuito Film Festival in NY.

He's also the co-creator and lead actor on the web series "America Loves Carlos" and creator, writer, lead actor and director of the web series "In The Act". He has directed music videos for established Argentine rock bands.

As a composer, his music has appeared on several episodes of the MTV show Catfish as well as World of Jenks & Caged. Commercial work includes music production for abc carpet & home-the New York staple department store-TV ads. His musical solo project, The Quiet Celebrations has released two EPs and a third one is currently in the works.

He lives in New York.

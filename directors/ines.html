Inés Vogelfang is a film and tv editor, and postproduction coordinator from Buenos Aires, Argentina. She studied Sound and Image Design at the University of Buenos Aires where she discovered her passion for documentary while making her 2009 non-fiction project Heart of Fantasy on children at a pediatric hospital.

She completed her graduate certificate on Documentary Film at The New School and she is now a Master of Arts candidate.

Her thesis film is a short documentary about soccer in New York City, a city symphony about what it feels to be part of the soccer community in downtown Manhattan. Her ongoing project is an interactive documentary about how soccer changes lives around the globe.

She speaks Spanish, French, Portuguese and English; and she will continue to study the world forever, finding different ways to tell the stories she discovers.

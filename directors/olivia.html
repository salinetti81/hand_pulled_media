Olivia Ebertz is a New-York based director and producer of sensitive ethnographic documentaries featuring unorthodox structure and subject matter. She just completed her first solo-directed film, Somos de Hueso. She holds a BA in Anthropology from Oberlin College and a Graduate Certificate in Documentary Filmmaking from the New School for Public Engagement.
